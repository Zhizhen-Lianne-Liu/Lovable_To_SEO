# Lovable prompt — V2 (Lovable-style, warm gradients, friendly)

Paste this into a fresh Lovable project. The result should feel like a sister product to Lovable itself — same visual family, so a Lovable founder lands here and instantly feels at home.

---

Build a single-page marketing site for **lovabletoseo** — an AI marketer for early-stage Lovable founders. The product takes a Lovable app's GitHub repo, converts the React SPA into static HTML, and enhances it with Peec AI buyer-query data, then opens a pull request with the rebuilt site. Target user: a non-technical founder who built a landing page in Lovable and just realized nobody finds it on Google or in ChatGPT. The voice should be warm, magic-feeling, low-jargon.

**Design direction.** Warm and soft. Background is a light cream-peach (`#fff7f4`), with two big radial gradients in the top corners — one peach (`#ffd9c2`), one pink (`#ffc8e0`) — fading into the cream. Primary accent gradient runs pink → peach → apricot (`#ff7eb6` → `#ff9472` → `#ffb46a`), used on the headline word that pops, on big CTA buttons, and on the floating agent character. White cards with very rounded corners (24px radius) and soft drop shadows (`0 12px 30px -12px rgba(74,34,56,.18)`). Typography is a friendly sans-serif (Inter, semibold for display, regular for body). Display sizes large but not as huge as a B&W aesthetic — line-height around 1.05. Use rounded-full pill shapes for buttons and small badges. Minimal emoji (one or two per section, used purposefully). Vibe: lovable.dev, mintlify, raycast.com.

**Page structure, top to bottom.**

1. Header. Left: a small floating "agent" — a 32px gradient circle with a `✨` emoji inside, gently floating up-and-down on a 3.5s loop — paired with the wordmark "lovabletoseo". Right: pill nav links "How it works / Why" + a gradient pill button "Scan free →".

2. **Hero, centered.** A small pill badge at the top: a soft white-with-blur card with `✨ Made for Lovable founders`. Then a giant centered headline: "Make your Lovable app" on one line, then "actually findable." in the gradient on a second line. Sub-line below in 70%-opacity dark text: "Your page looks great. Google can't see it. ChatGPT has nothing to quote. We fix both — in one PR you can review and merge." Then a big rounded "input pill" — fully white, rounded-full, with a subtle gradient border (achieved by a layered linear-gradient background-clip trick), with `https://` prefix in muted text on the left, an input field with placeholder `receiptly.lovable.app`, and a gradient pill button "Scan my app". Below the input, dimmed text: "Free. ~10 seconds. We don't store your URL." Below that, a floating decoration: a 64px gradient agent circle with `✨` emoji, floating up and down, with three pink sparkle particles fading in and out around it on different delays.

3. **Scan flow** (hidden until the user submits the form).
   - Loading state: a white rounded card. Top of card has a 48px gradient agent circle with `🪄` emoji and the text "Scanning <domain>" in semibold + "This is the magic part." in muted text. Below, a list of steps with thinking-dot animations (three small pink/peach/apricot pulsing dots) that resolve to small pink checkmarks. Steps: "Cloning your repo…", "Spotting your tech stack…", "Reading every component…", "Asking Peec what buyers query…", "Comparing to top-cited rivals…". 600ms per step.
   - Result state: centered above the metric cards, a 56px agent circle with `😬` emoji and a friendly headline: "Oof. Here's what we found at `<domain>`." with the domain styled in the accent gradient. Sub: "Don't worry. This is normal for a Lovable app. It's also fixable in one PR." Then three side-by-side rounded white cards, each with a label, a giant counter that animates from 0 to its final value, and a one-line description. Card 1: "Indexable by Google — 0% — Your page renders client-side. Crawlers see nothing." Card 2: "Mentioned in ChatGPT — 1% — vs QuickBooks 51% · Wave 28%". Card 3: "Schema blocks present — 0/5 — No JSON-LD, no FAQ markup, no comparison data." Below, a final rounded card with a 48px `🚀` agent circle, headline "Connect GitHub. We open the PR." and one paragraph explaining the flow. Gradient pill button "Connect GitHub" with the GitHub mark icon.

4. **How it works section.** Centered. Top pill badge `🪄 The pipeline`. Headline "Five steps. One agent. Your PR." Sub: "No black box — every step writes a file you can read." Below, a single big rounded card containing a 5-column grid (5 stages). Each column is centered: a 64px gradient agent circle with one emoji on top (📥 / ⚡ / 🔍 / ✨ / 🚀), then a step name (Ingest / Prerender / Diagnose / Enhance / Ship), then a one-liner ("Clone your repo. Detect Vite + React." / "Convert your SPA into static HTML." / "Ask Peec what buyers query." / "Layer FAQ, schema, comparison." / "Open a PR on a fresh branch."). **Crucial animation:** a 14px gradient orb — pink-to-peach with a peach glow — travels horizontally across the card on a 7-second loop, lingering briefly at each step (use a CSS keyframe at 0/20/40/60/80/100%). Cards should fade-up on scroll.

5. **"Why this works" section.** Centered headline "Why this works." Sub: "Every recommendation is grounded in real Peec data, not hunches." Below, a 2x2 grid of rounded white cards. Each card: one large emoji at the top (🧠, 🏆, 📊, 💝), a semibold heading, and one short paragraph. (1) "Real buyer queries — Peec records the exact things buyers type into ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini. We target those, verbatim." (2) "Citation-shaped content — We see which URLs LLMs already cite. Then we shape your page to fit that pattern." (3) "Competitor gap data — Peec shows where rivals out-cite you. We attack the largest gap first." (4) "Lovable stays the editor — We don't touch your React source. Output goes to a new branch under `seo/`."

6. **Testimonial section.** Centered. A single big rounded card with a faint giant `"` decoration in the top-right corner. A large display-weight quote: "I shipped a landing page on Saturday. By Wednesday I realized nobody was finding it. lovabletoseo opened the PR Friday." Below the quote, a 40px agent circle with `👋` and the attribution: "a Lovable founder / probably you, soon" in two lines.

7. **Final CTA section.** Centered, big. Headline: "Get your Lovable app found." with "Lovable app" in the accent gradient. Sub: "One PR, ten minutes, no migration. Free during the hackathon." Big gradient pill button: "Scan my Lovable app →".

8. **Footer.** Subtle top border (`rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`). Left: small agent circle + wordmark. Right: "built for the Big Berlin Hack — Peec AI track" in muted text.

**Tone notes.** Friendly, magical, low-jargon. We're talking to a founder who doesn't think of themselves as a developer. Use words like "magic", "found", "actually", "your call", "no migration". Avoid acronyms early (introduce GEO and SPA only after explaining what they mean in context). Keep emoji to about one per section.

**Interactions.** The agent floating in the hero is the signature visual moment — make sure the float and the sparkle particles feel alive but not noisy. The orb traveling through the pipeline is the secondary moment. Counter animations on the scan results should feel snappy (~900ms). Cards should fade-up subtly on scroll.

The Connect GitHub button should be a placeholder that opens a small modal saying "GitHub OAuth handshake — coming soon" for now.
