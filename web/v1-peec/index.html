/ FAQ

Honest answers.

Will this break my Lovable app?
No. We only add files under seo/ on a new branch. Your existing React source is untouched. Merge or close — your call.
Do I have to migrate off Lovable?
No. Lovable stays the editor. We deploy the static site on a different subdomain (e.g. www.yourbrand.com) while the editable app stays at yourbrand.lovable.app.
How is this different from LovableHTML?
LovableHTML prerenders what's there. We rewrite the content itself based on Peec data — what queries buyers actually ask and what content LLMs actually cite. Indexable vs cited is the gap we close.
What does GEO mean?
Generative Engine Optimization. Becoming the source LLMs quote when a buyer asks for a recommendation. Different playbook than classic SEO — extractable answers, citable stats, schema markup.
